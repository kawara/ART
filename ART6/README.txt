ART v6 (Autonomous Robot Toy)
Mechanical base: Remote control toy car
Motor control: microcontroller manipulation of the RC control
Sensors: five E18-D80NK infrared (obstacle detection, no distance ranging)
Computer: Arduino Uno
Characteristics: hacked inexpensive RC car, self-driving, forward and reverse fast interrupt-driven collision detection
Cost to build: ~425 RMB (~USD$65)
Code: https://github.com/rngadam/ART/tree/master/ART6
